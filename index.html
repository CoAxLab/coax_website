Research Areas

Brain Network Architecture and Cognition

TIM TO EDIT IF NEEDED: How does the topographic organization of neural pathways provide insight into underlying computational properties? How do individual differences in the integrity of connections (both functional and structural) in large-scale neural systems predict differences in brain function or cognitive performance?.

Statistical Properties of Sensorimotor learning

TIM TO EDIT IF NEEDED: What are the computational properties of sensorimotor learning at multiple time scales (e.g., across milliseconds or days) and spatial resolutions (e.g. small cortical networks vs. multi-region brain networks)? How can the properties of learning be leveraged in applications outside the laboratory (e.g., professional sports).?.

Response Planning and Stopping

TIM TO EDIT IF NEEDED: How does context influence the way we plan or inhibit our actions? Are there different neural systems and cognitive strategies for proactive versus reactive planning (or stopping)?
